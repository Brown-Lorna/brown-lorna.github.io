Full-time Temple Missionary Suites

Stay near the temple and have other missionaries for neighbors. These modern and well-maintained suites provides a quiet atmosphere, and are in a separate building from the inn. The suites are furnished, but there is room to personalize your suite by adding a few pieces. Suites are available on a month to month rental only for full-time temple missionaries.

Amenities included with the suites:

  • Kitchenette stocked with pots, dishes, and flatware
  • King bed in separate bedroom
  • Furnished living/dining room
  • Private bathroom with tub/shower
  • Weekly linen service available
  • Shared lounge area
  • Access to hotel pool and exercise room
  • Shared laundry facilities
  • Shuttle to and from the temple available for all shift changes
