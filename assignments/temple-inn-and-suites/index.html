About the Fort Lauderdale Temple

The beautiful Fort Lauderdale Temple is located in Davie, Florida. It was dedicated May 4, 2014. It's unique decor reflects the southern Florida landscape. It is open to temple recommend-holding members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Fort Lauderdale temple is one of the largest of the small temples. It doesn't have rental clothing, but out-of-the-area patrons can borrow clothing if necessary. There is also no visitor center, but visitors are welcome to tour the grounds on their own. The temple is open 5 days a week with a limited schedule.
