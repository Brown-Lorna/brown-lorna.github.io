Wedding Reception Hall

The Temple Inn & Suites Wedding Reception Hall offers a unique experience. The wedding party and their guests can attend the temple, then go to the reception without having to travel in between. The inn offers rooms where guests can stay the night for their convenience.

The Wedding Reception Hall holds up to 300 guests.

The Inn offers the following choices for a wedding reception in the hall.

  • Bride's room to prepare for the reception
  • Tables and chairs
  • Table linens
  • Sound system, including a microphone
  • Early access to the hall for decorating
  • Our china or your dishes
  • Custodial clean up available

Catering Options

You may use your own caterer, have access to our kitchen, or use our on-site catering

On-site catering options include:

  • Wedding cake
  • Groom's cake
  • Fruit and vegetable trays
  • Hor d'oeuvres
  • Punch, water, and sodas
